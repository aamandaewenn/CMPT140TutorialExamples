Algorithm EmptyDishwasher:
1. Open the dishwasher
2. Open bottom rack drawer
while there are still dishes in bottom rack:
	3. Take out dish from bottom rack
	4. Put dish Away
5. Close the bottom rack drawer
6. Open the top rack drawer
while there are still dishes in top rack:
	8. Take out dish from the top rack
	9. Put dish away
10. Close the top rack drawer
11. Close the dishwasher
